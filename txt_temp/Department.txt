The University of British Columbia (UBC) is one of Canada's leading universities, known for its research and academic excellence. It has numerous departments across various faculties. Below is a list of some major faculties and their respective departments:

### 1. Faculty of Arts
- **Departments Include:**
  - Anthropology
  - Archaeology
  - Economics
  - English Language and Literatures
  - History
  - Philosophy
  - Political Science
  - Psychology
  - Sociology

### 2. Faculty of Science
- **Departments Include:**
  - Biology
  - Chemistry
  - Computer Science
  - Mathematics
  - Physics and Astronomy
  - Earth, Ocean and Atmospheric Sciences (EOAS)
  - Statistics

### 3. Sauder School of Business
- **Departments Include:**
  - Accounting
  - Finance
  - Marketing
  - Organizational Behavior and Human Resources
  - Strategy and Business Economics
  - Operations and Logistics

### 4. Faculty of Applied Science
- **Departments Include:**
  - Civil Engineering
  - Electrical and Computer Engineering
  - Mechanical Engineering
  - Chemical and Biological Engineering
  - Materials Engineering
  - Mining Engineering
  - Environmental Engineering

### 5. Faculty of Medicine
- **Departments Include:**
  - Anatomy and Cell Biology
  - Biochemistry and Molecular Biology
  - Family Practice
  - Pathology and Laboratory Medicine
  - Pediatrics
  - Psychiatry
  - Surgery

### 6. Faculty of Education
- **Departments Include:**
  - Educational and Counseling Psychology
  - Educational Studies
  - Curriculum and Pedagogy
  - Language and Literacy Education
  - Special Education

### 7. Faculty of Law
- **Departments Include:**
  - Common Law
  - Indigenous Legal Studies
  - Environmental Law
  - International Law

### 8. Faculty of Land and Food Systems
- **Departments Include:**
  - Food, Nutrition and Health
  - Land and Water Systems
  - Sustainable Agriculture and Food Systems

### 9. School of Nursing
- Programs focusing on nursing education, midwifery, and healthcare leadership.

### 10. School of Music
- Offers programs focusing on performance, composition, and music education.

### 11. School of Architecture and Landscape Architecture (SALA)
- Focuses on architecture and landscape architecture programs.

### 12. Faculty of Forestry
- Includes departments focusing on forest resources management, wood science, and forest ecology.

### 13. Faculty of Pharmaceutical Sciences
- Focuses on drug development, healthcare, and pharmaceutical sciences.

### 14. Faculty of Dentistry
- Offers programs focusing on oral health, dental hygiene, and dentistry.

### 15. Faculty of Kinesiology
- Studies physical activity, health, and the science of human movement.

### 16. School of Community and Regional Planning (SCARP)
- Focuses on community planning, urban design, and sustainable development.
